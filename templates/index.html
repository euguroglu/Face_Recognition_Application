about

Mechanical and Mechatronic Engineer(M.Sc.) with 9+ years experince in automotive and aeronautics industry on control systems, robotics, computer vision and data science. He is currently working at Farplas Automotive Company as Digital Transformation Team Leader.

He also continue his academic carrier at Istanbul Technical University as PhD canditate on Mechatronics Enginnering. He developed rotational 6 degrees of freedom parallel manipulator with unique kinematic and dynamic capabilities on light workloads. Currently he is focusing state of the art detection and visual classification tasks using deep learning techniques.
