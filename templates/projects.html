Person Detection

Interesting image filter effects, such as a pencil sketch or a cartoonizer effect, do not have to be very computationally involved to look good. In fact, in order to create a beautiful black-and-white pencil sketch effect, all you essentially need is first detect person from the image to apply strong filtering to them after some blurring and two image blending techniques called dodging and burning.
